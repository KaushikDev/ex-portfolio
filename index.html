Introduction

Hey, it is good to meet you. I am Piyush Kaushik and I am the designer and developer of this portfolio website. Based out of Noida, India, I am passionate about learning front-end web development. My definite goal in life is to give back to community in any manner possible and I found web development may actually help me achieve this goal.

Why Web Development

With more than 6 years of experience in software testing domain, I always felt like I could contribute more towards development and hence started to pursue my dream goal. I know the road isn't easy, but overcoming challenges is what gives me satisfaction. I strongly believe that there is nothing that you cannot learn and it is never too late to learn. One of my favorite quotations is, "From humble beginnings come great things". You get the idea.

What have I done so far?

I started by learning basics of web development, the big three viz. HTML, CSS and Javascript. I completed Responsive Web Design certification from FCC and Javascript Basics and Accelerated ES6 Javascript certifications from Udemy. I am currently doing more FCC certifications and building projects along the way. You're welcome to check out the projects section for projects I've built. I am active on and and regularly build some fun stuff.

Let's work together.

If you like my work and would like me to work with you, feel free to drop me an or reach out on my or profiles.
